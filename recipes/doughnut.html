Doughnut Recipe

Classic homemade doughnuts, soft and delicious!

Ingredients

Name Amount
All-purpose flour2 cups
Sugar1/2 cup
Salt1/2 teaspoon
Baking powder1 tablespoon
Ground nutmeg1/2 teaspoon
Milk1/2 cup
Large eggs2
Melted butter1/4 cup
Oil for fryingas needed

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and nutmeg.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Scoop dough into rings and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Drain on paper towels and dust with powdered sugar or glaze as desired.
