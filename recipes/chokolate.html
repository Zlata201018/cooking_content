Chocolate Recipe

Easy homemade chocolate with just a few ingredients!

Ingredients

Name Amount
Cacao butter (food-grade)as needed (about 1/2 cup for a small batch)
Cocoa powder (unsweetened) or cacao powder1/2 cup
Maple syrup2-4 tbsp (to taste)
Milk powder (optional, for milk chocolate)2 tbsp
Vanilla extract (optional)1/2 tsp

Note: You can use coconut oil instead of cacao butter, but the chocolate will melt faster. For vegan milk chocolate, use coconut, soy, or rice milk powder.

Instructions

  1. Grate or finely chop the cacao butter and melt it over a double boiler, stirring/whisking often. Once melted, remove it from the heat.
    To prepare a double boiler: Add an inch of water to a small-medium saucepan, bring to a simmer, and top with a heat-proof bowl, ensuring it doesn’t touch the water.
  2. Stir in the cocoa powder, maple syrup, and vanilla (if using). Whisk until smooth. Optionally add the milk powder for homemade milk chocolate.
  3. Pour the homemade chocolate mixture into silicone molds (use a silicone spatula to help get it all out of the bowl).
  4. Let it set for several hours in the fridge or 20-30 minutes in the freezer.
  5. When ready, release the homemade chocolate bars/shapes from the molds and enjoy!
